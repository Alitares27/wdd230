About Us

we are a small business that specializes in locally grown organic foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, plus we have food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. we sell fruits directly from farmers in the area, but also from some national and international suppliers

Our Mission

our mission is to provide organic, fresh and healthy products, prepared at the moment, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the entire local population including tourists visiting the coastal areas.
