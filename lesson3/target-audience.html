SCENARIOS

  • Andrew is a taxi driver who has a hard time getting customers without avoiding spending a lot of fuel, he is constantly looking for options to use fuel more efficiently.

  • Kate is an accountant who is often busy, she arrives at work early in the morning and sometimes leaves late at night, she has trouble finding a reliable taxi service that won't waste her time, many taxi drivers try to take advantage of night hours to charging you too high a fee, you have tried various apps and local taxi services

  • Andrew is paying a loan he got to buy his car, last month he couldn't pay the loan on time, because he didn't have enough money, this caused an increase in interest.

  • Kate has a 12-year-old daughter, she is a single mother and she needs someone to pick her daughter up from school to take her to work, but she doesn't trust taxi drivers because she was robbed by a fake taxi driver months ago.

  • Andrew has a large family, this is the reason why he seeks to save money and have more clients that allow him to have enough money to pay the loan and support his family.
